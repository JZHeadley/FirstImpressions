Finding a Job

It can be a daunting task to get back on your feet and find work after being released from prison. However, many quality companies across the nation hire workers with criminal backgrounds to assist with reintegration into their communities. Our database allows you to easily locate these jop openings in your area and get in direct contact with hiring managers. If you would like assistance building your resume before applying to a job, please visit our Resources page.

Reentry Programs

Find reentry programs near you to help you concentrate on adjusting to life on the outside. Many programs offer short term housing, job assistance and often have other spiritual and therapy aspects within the program.
